Garden Gordon

A charming 2D gardening game with artwork made from hand cut paper.

About

Another ongoing project I'm working on - this time a collaborative effort with artist Ollie Malin.

Garden Gordon is a charming 2D game with artwork made from hand-cut paper. You play as a gardener named Gordon who, whilst tending to his plants, must defend them from mischievous creatures. The game takes place over the course of a year, with one day spent in each of the seasons, each with a unique colour palette. Having sown the seeds in spring, Gordon must nurture and protect his crop through the hot and dry summer by fending off critters with his trusty watering can. A successful autumn harvest will provide Gordon with a wholesome and tasty stew to see him through the winter and into the following year feeling healthy and nourished, where more foes await ready to steal his vegetables.

My Role

The idea for this project was co-conceived together with Ollie. I am handling all the coding, audio programming, project management, and source control. Ollie is taking care of the art and the composition of original music.

Tools

This game is built in Unity, with scripts written in C#. FMOD is the audio engine.
