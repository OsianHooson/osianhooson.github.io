Space Shooter

My take on the classic asteroids game.

Inspired by the classics

After playing Asteroids on a proper old arcade cabinet at the Four Quarters in Peckham I decided I'd make my own version in Unity. Shoot asteroids with the ship's laser to release minerals. Collect the minerals to score points. Easy peasy. This one comes with original music.

How many minerals can you collect before times up?

  • Play now

Tools

This project was built in Unity, with all scripting done in C#. The disc that surrounds the ship that represents the player's vision was based on a tutorial by Sebastian Lague which uses a procedural mesh to simulate shadows from a light source. I also composed the music and sound FX for this one.
