Space Shooter

My take on the classic asteroids game.

Inspired by the classics

After playing Asteroids on a proper old arcade cabinet at the Four Quarters in Peckham I decided I'd make my own version.

I wanted to play with the idea of limiting the player's vision in some way. The disk that surrounds the ship represents the players' field of view. It grows as more minerals are collected, and shrinks as the ship is damaged.

How many minerals can you collect before times up?

Shoot asteroids with the ship's laser to release minerals. Collect the minerals to score points. Don't get hit by the asteroids. Easy peasy.

  • Play now

Tools and Workflow

This project was built in Unity, with all scripting done in C#.

I used an image processing shader to alter the final colour and provide that pink tint. I composed the music and sound FX for this one. The field of view disk is a procedural mesh based on a tutorial by Sebastian Lague.
