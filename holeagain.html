Hole Again

2D Pothole fixing game for Global Game Jam.

My Role

Here's a game that I made in 48 hours as part of a team of two coders, one artist, one animator and two project leaders / producers. In this project I was responsible for coding the collision, player interaction, player movement, player health, and audio systems. I also assembled and organised the assets in Unity and sequenced the flow of the program.

Tools and Workflow

This project was built in Unity. Scripting all done in C#.

Working with another coder this time around was great. Bouncing technical ideas back and forth and solving problems collaboratively was really fun. The producers were also super handy to have on a project with such a short time frame. They helped keep scope in check and were there to keep everyone focussed on the brief.
